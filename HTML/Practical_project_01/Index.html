Hello, my name is Dalius I am 29 years old and live in Druskininkai, Lithuania. I have been interested in compiuters since childhood, for me it was gadget where I spend endless hours discovering new things, comunicating with others and playing various video games.
First experience with coding I faced when I was 13 years old.
In that time was in 7th grade of school. School's IT teacher at that time have been teaching us basic programming with "Pascal" language for me it was totally garbage. As for homework we got simple tasks as draw arious shapes like triangles, squares, circles and had to fill up with color it was headache for me becouse we have done that in home in assigment book and later on lesson time write out into compiuter to compile. I mostly had made simple syntax errors, luckly I had very smart classmate who sat next to me and point it out for me.
Next year I had more pleasant experience. It was very simple experience and basically I can't call it as coding. However, I liked it I spend many night doing it. By that time one of most popular game was Grand theft auto: San Andreas and as completed it many times it get bored, so I started to play it online.
Afterwards I found some server files to download and host it as mine it was written in pawn language. So becouse I was newbe in coding, mine task was simple very simple I just edit script file. Acctually only thing I did was changing lines from english to Lithuanian and host it. That was one of few hosted lithuanian servers, but no so long after hype went down and I abandoned that.
Later, I started play others online games, one of them - Runescape. So, I also find some servers for download and did same thing. But auditory was humble so just only few people was playing.
Most people who know me thought my future belong with programming, but I was the one who made wrong decision. So I decided choose other way, that made my future more uncertain...
I started study the subject which totaly unsuits for my personality and goals...
And now I am here and my new journey to IT starts from here. Thats all for this time. See ya soon.
